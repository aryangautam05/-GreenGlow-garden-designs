Cookies Policy

Welcome to GreenGlow Garden Designs’ comprehensive Cookies Policy. This detailed statement explains how we utilize cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website to improve your experience, analyze usage, and provide personalized content.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by websites you visit. They enable the website to recognize your device and store information such as preferences or login status.

There are different types of cookies used for various purposes:

  • Session Cookies: These are temporary cookies erased once you close your browser.
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device for a set period, remembering preferences for your next visit.
  • First-Party Cookies: Set by our website itself to store your settings and session information.
  • Third-Party Cookies: Set by external services we integrate for analytics, advertising, or customer experience enhancement.

How We Use Cookies

Our use of cookies enables us to provide you with a seamless, efficient, and personalized browsing and gardening service experience. Cookies help us to:

  • Remember your privacy preferences and cookie consent choices.
  • Ensure secure logins and maintain session continuity.
  • Analyze user behavior and website performance to improve service quality.
  • Deliver personalized content, offers, and advertisements relevant to your interests.
  • Enhance website functionality such as language preferences, shopping cart contents, and interactive features.

Each cookie serves a specific function, and you can choose your preferred level of cookie acceptance.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are vital for the operation of our website. They support features like page navigation, user authentication, and security operations. Disabling these could impact your use of the site.

Performance and Analytics Cookies

These cookies collect information on how visitors use our site, such as pages visited and errors encountered. This information helps us understand user engagement and optimize the site’s performance.

Functionality Cookies

Functionality cookies allow our website to remember choices you’ve made, such as your preferred language or region, improving your experience upon return visits.

Advertising and Targeting Cookies

Advertising cookies track user behavior across websites to deliver relevant advertisements tailored to your interests, enhancing your experience but also for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Cookies and Services

We may employ third-party services such as Google Analytics, social media platforms, and advertising networks, which place their own cookies through our site.

These third parties operate under their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them to understand their data handling practices.

We do not control these third-party cookies but ensure our partners adhere to strict privacy standards.

Managing and Controlling Cookies

You have the right to control cookies. You can accept or decline cookies using the cookie consent dialog below, or by adjusting your web browser settings.

Most browsers allow you to manage cookie settings through preferences or settings menus where you can:

  • View what cookies are stored on your device.
  • Delete cookies individually or in bulk.
  • Block all or third-party cookies.

Please note blocking cookies may limit some website functions and personalized experiences.

Cookie Duration and Expiry

The lifespan of cookies varies by type:

  • Session Cookies: Expire once you close your browser.
  • Persistent Cookies: Can remain for days, months or even years depending on their function.

We review and update our cookie usage periodically for optimal performance and compliance.
