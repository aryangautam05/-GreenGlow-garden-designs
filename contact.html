How to Schedule a Consultation

Scheduling a consultation with GreenGlow is easy to start the journey toward your dream garden:

  • Contact us via phone or email to request availability.
  • Provide some details about your property and garden preferences.
  • We will confirm the consultation date, time, and necessary preparations.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Do you service areas outside New York?

Primarily, we serve New York City and nearby areas. For projects farther afield, please contact us to discuss possibilities.

Can you help with ecological or sustainable garden designs?

Absolutely! Ecological stewardship guides many of our designs incorporating native plants, water conservation, and wildlife habitat features.

What is your process for garden maintenance?

We offer flexible maintenance packages tailored to your garden’s size and needs, from weekly visits to seasonal care.

How long do garden design projects take?

Project duration varies by scope but typically ranges from 4-12 weeks from design to installation.

Can you work with limited outdoor spaces?

Yes, we specialize in maximizing small urban gardens, balcony designs, and container planting.
